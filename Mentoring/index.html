Mentoring

I supervise students in functional analysis, partial differential equations, variational methods, convex optimization and deep unfolding networks with applications in image processing, remote sensing and computer vision.

If you are a BSc/MSc/PhD student seeking a research internship or a student at UIB looking for a possible topic for your degree/master thesis, please contact me!

You can find below the list of PhD, undergraduate and visiting students I have supervised in research, and the MSc and BSc students whose thesis I have advised.
